## 📖 Original Description
As a Reporting/Data Consumer (internal analytics + external reporting)
I want a secure, consumable dataset/feed for leads oversight
So that I can reliably build dashboards and reports without manual data extraction.


## 🎯 Acceptance Criteria (验收标准)

Feed is available and accessible to authorised consumers
* * Each record includes campaign and lead identifiers
Data can be consumed for reporting without manual intervention
